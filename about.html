Who We Are

Established in 1984, Haldar & Associates, Chartered Accountants is a single window professional services firm on providing high quality services one needs from a business advisory organization. Haldar & Associates was found and promoted by the highly accomplished Chartered Accountant and Partner – CA Ashim Kr Haldar.

We are a firm of Chartered Accountants, serving Indian and multinational corporates, banking and financial institutions, start-ups, high net-worth individuals, executives and expatriates.

We believe to stay ahead in the vibrant changing world economy to provide the most consistent and prompt quality services. This philosophy is backed by experienced and motivated professionals with matching expertise. We conduct regular in-house training programmes to stay updated with the changing laws and practices. So, we not only provide you with business solutions under one roof but also regularly and timely upgrade knowledge and increase awareness.
